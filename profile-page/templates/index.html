Why we call it as Spectrum Care?

Autism is known as a “spectrum” disorder because there is wide variation in the type and severity of symptoms people experience.

And here we are caring for that spectrum...

What we do?

At Spectrum Care, our mission is to revolutionize the way autism is detected and treated. We believe that early and accurate detection is crucial for providing children with the support they need to thrive. Through the use of cutting-edge eye-tracking and facial image analysis technology, combined with personalized intervention plans, we aim to make a meaningful difference in the lives of children and their families.
